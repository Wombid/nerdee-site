Privacy Policy

Effective 19 July 2026. Issued by Wombid Pty Ltd trading as Nerdee (Australia). Contact: hello@nerdee.com.au

This policy covers the SteerPack for Jira app and this website. Wombid Pty Ltd is bound by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

The app: your data never reaches us

SteerPack runs entirely on Atlassian's Forge platform. All app data is stored in Forge hosted storage inside your own Atlassian site and never leaves Atlassian infrastructure. The app makes no external network calls: no third party services, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising technology, no external fonts. We have no ability to access, view or export any data the app stores.

What the app stores, inside your site

Program configuration (workstreams, milestones, thresholds), immutable pack snapshots of Jira delivery data (issue keys, summaries, assignee display names, dates) and PM assessments (status overrides and written notes). This can include personal data such as user display names drawn from your Jira site. This data remains under your organisation's control at all times and follows your site's Atlassian data residency region.

Access control

All Jira and Confluence access runs as the person using the app. SteerPack can never read or publish anything that person could not read or publish themselves.

Retention and deletion

App data follows Atlassian's Forge storage lifecycle: uninstalling the app removes access to stored data, and deletion is handled by Atlassian under its platform policies. Your site admins control the app's installation at all times.

This website

This website is a static site. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics and collects no personal information. It is served through Cloudflare, whose infrastructure logs standard web requests to operate the service. Fonts are served by Google Fonts, which receives standard web request data when pages load.

Support email

If you email hello@nerdee.com.au we receive your address and message. Support mail is handled in Google Workspace by Wombid Pty Ltd, used only to respond to you, kept only as long as needed for support and legal obligations, and never sold or shared for marketing. Do not include site data in support messages unless we ask.

Marketplace purchases

Billing is handled entirely by Atlassian under its own privacy policy. We receive licensing and sales reporting from Atlassian (organisation name and licence details), not payment details.

Overseas disclosure

Where your Atlassian site is hosted determines where app data resides. Our support mail is processed by Google Workspace, which may store data outside Australia.

Data breaches

We comply with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme under the Privacy Act. Because the app gives us no access to your data, a breach of our systems cannot expose your app data.

Your rights and complaints

You may request access to or correction of personal information we hold (in practice, support correspondence) by emailing hello@nerdee.com.au. We respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied, you may complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (oaic.gov.au). EU and UK users may also have rights under the GDPR; because the app sends us no personal data, requests generally sit with your own organisation as controller of your Atlassian site.

Changes

We will post any changes here with a new effective date. Material changes are noted on the Marketplace listing.
