SteerPack Privacy Policy

Effective: [date]. Publisher: Nerdee, a business name of Wombid Pty Ltd (Australia). Contact: hello@nerdee.com.au

SteerPack runs entirely on Atlassian's Forge platform. All app data is stored in Forge storage inside your own Atlassian cloud site and never leaves Atlassian infrastructure.

The app makes no external network calls: no third-party services, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising technology, no external fonts.

Data the app stores: your program configuration (workstreams, milestones, thresholds), immutable pack snapshots of Jira delivery data (issue keys, summaries, assignee display names, dates) and PM assessments (status overrides and written notes, which may include names). This may include personal data such as user display names drawn from your Jira site.

Access: all Jira and Confluence access runs as the person using the app; SteerPack can never read or publish anything that person could not.

We (the publisher) receive no customer data of any kind. We cannot see, access or export your site's data.

Retention and deletion follow Atlassian's Forge storage lifecycle: uninstalling the app removes access to stored data, and deletion is handled by Atlassian per their platform policies.

Support email (hello@nerdee.com.au) is handled in Google Workspace by Wombid Pty Ltd; do not include site data in support messages unless asked.

Questions or complaints: hello@nerdee.com.au. Australian Privacy Principles apply.
