Privacy Policy

Effective 19 July 2026. Issued by Wombid Pty Ltd trading as Nerdee (Australia). Contact: hello@nerdee.com.au

This policy covers the SteerPack for Jira app and this website. Wombid Pty Ltd is a small business that the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) does not currently require to comply with the Australian Privacy Principles. We commit voluntarily to handling personal information in line with those principles, and this policy is written and applied to that standard. If we opt in to coverage under the Act, or the Act comes to apply to us, we will update this policy to say so.

The app: your data never reaches us

SteerPack runs entirely on Atlassian's Forge platform. All app data is stored in Forge hosted storage inside your own Atlassian site and never leaves Atlassian infrastructure. The app makes no external network calls: no third party services, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising technology, no external fonts. Nothing in the app sends data to us and we operate no servers that hold it, so we have no routine means to access, view or export any data the app stores.

What the app stores, inside your site

Program configuration (workstreams, milestones, thresholds), immutable pack snapshots of Jira delivery data (issue keys, summaries, assignee display names, dates) and PM assessments (status overrides and written notes). This can include personal data such as user display names drawn from your Jira site. This data remains under your organisation's control at all times and follows your site's Atlassian data residency region.

Access control

All Jira and Confluence access runs as the person using the app. SteerPack can never read or publish anything that person could not read or publish themselves.

Retention and deletion

App data follows Atlassian's Forge storage lifecycle. Uninstalling the app removes access to stored data immediately. Atlassian then retains the stored data for 28 days before deleting it; during that period it can be recovered only through a request made with your consent and initiated within 21 days of uninstall. Your site admins control the app's installation at all times, and Atlassian's current Forge storage documentation is authoritative on this lifecycle.

This website

This website is a static site. Wombid sets no cookies and runs no analytics or advertising technology on it, and we do not use it to collect personal information for ourselves. Serving it does involve two providers that process standard web request data, meaning your IP address, browser and device details, and the pages you request: Cloudflare, which hosts and delivers the site and keeps operational logs to run and secure the service, and Google, whose Google Fonts service supplies the site's typefaces and receives the same request data when pages load. Both providers process this data to deliver and secure the service, and both operate globally, including in the United States.

Support email

If you email hello@nerdee.com.au we receive your address and message. Support mail is handled in Google Workspace by Wombid Pty Ltd, used only to respond to you, kept only as long as needed for support and legal obligations, and never sold or shared for marketing. Do not include site data in support messages unless we ask.

Marketplace purchases

Billing is handled entirely by Atlassian under its own privacy policy. We receive licensing and sales reporting from Atlassian (organisation name and licence details), not payment details.

Overseas disclosure

Where your Atlassian site is hosted determines where app data resides. Outside the app, our providers operate globally: Cloudflare (website hosting and delivery) and Google (website fonts, and Google Workspace for support mail) are likely to process the website and email data described above in the United States and other countries in which they operate.

Data breaches

On the same voluntary basis as the rest of this policy, if a data breach involving personal information we hold is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify the people affected and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner in line with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme. App data sits inside Atlassian infrastructure and none of it flows to our systems, so a breach of this website or our support mailbox would not reach it. If an incident on our side ever put customer data at risk, we would assess and notify it under the same commitment.

Your rights and complaints

You may request access to or correction of personal information we hold (in practice, support correspondence) by emailing hello@nerdee.com.au. We respond within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, tell us and we will escalate it within Wombid. The Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (oaic.gov.au) publishes guidance on privacy complaints; if the Privacy Act covers Wombid at the relevant time, you may also complain to the OAIC. EU and UK users may also have rights under the GDPR; because the app sends us no personal data, requests generally sit with your own organisation as controller of your Atlassian site.

Changes

We will post any changes here with a new effective date. Material changes are noted on the Marketplace listing.
