SteerPack for Jira

Board-ready steering committee packs, straight from your Jira delivery data

Nerdee builds opinionated delivery tools for Jira, made by Wombid Pty Ltd in Australia. Our first app, SteerPack, turns live Jira delivery data into a one-page A4 steering pack, a Confluence page and an editable two-slide PowerPoint insert.

Deterministic status, honest provenance.

RAG status is proposed by clear rules. A project manager can override it, but only with a written reason, and every pack shows which is which.

Immutable packs, real movement.

Every pack is a frozen snapshot. Each new pack shows exactly what changed since the last one: statuses, new risks, slipped milestones.

Your data never leaves Atlassian.

SteerPack runs entirely inside your Atlassian site on Forge, with no external services, no analytics and no data egress of any kind.
